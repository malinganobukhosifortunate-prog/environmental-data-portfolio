## Selected Projects

### 1. Wetland Water Quality & Aquatic Vegetation Assessment
**Focus:** Water quality, wetlands, environmental monitoring  
**Tools:** GIS, Remote Sensing, Excel, Python  

I worked on assessing water quality and aquatic vegetation health in a Ramsar-listed wetland.
The project combined field sampling with spatial analysis to understand environmental conditions
and support sustainable wetland management.

**Development relevance:**  
Water quality and wetland health are closely linked to livelihoods, ecosystem services,
and climate resilience in developing regions.
